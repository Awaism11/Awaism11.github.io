// About Me

I am a materials scientist specialised in spectroscopic imaging and analytical techniques for bioproducts characterization. My expertise includes FTIR, UV/VIS/NIR, and Raman hyperspectral imaging, combined with chemometrics and machine learning applications for spectral data analysis.

I earned my Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from Aalto University in 2023, with distinction (Top 20%), focusing on hyperspectral imaging and chemometrics for investigating chemical wood modification.

// Research Interests

> Hyperspectral Imaging

Near-infrared and chemical imaging techniques for wood characterization

> Chemometrics & Data Analysis

Multivariate analysis, machine learning, and imaging processing

> Fire Performance of Wood

Fire retardant treatments and durability assessment of modified wood

> Wood Modification

Chemical modification techniques including acetylation and resin impregnation

> Advanced Spectroscopy

Raman spectroscopy, nano-IR, XRF, and multimodal imaging

> Wood-Water Relations

Moisture dynamics and hygroscopic properties of modified wood
